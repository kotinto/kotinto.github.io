Equation of state that was used to obtain ground-state parameters $B_0$, $V_0$, $B_0^\prime$ and $E_0$:

$$ E(V) = E_0 + \frac{2 B_0 V_0}{(B_0^\prime-1)^2} \times \left( 2 - \left[5 + 3 B_0^\prime\left( \left(\frac{V}{V_0}\right)^{1/3} -1\right) - 3 \left(\frac{V}{V_0}\right)^{1/3} \right] \exp\left(-\frac{3}{2}\left(B_0^\prime-1\right)\left( \left(\frac{V}{V_0}\right)^{1/3} - 1\right)\right) \right) $$
